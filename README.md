## üß† What the Organizer Does
- Totals **W-2 wages** and **federal withholding**  
- Totals **1099 interest**, dividends, capital gains, and misc income  
- Computes **total income**  
- Helps you evaluate **standard vs. itemized deduction**  
- Includes logic for:
  - SALT deduction cap ($10,000)
  - Medical expenses over 7.5% of income  
  - Charitable contributions  
  - Mortgage interest  
- Calculates **taxable income**  
- Summarizes **all withholding**  
- Provides a **mapping table** so you can transfer values directly into IRS Form 1040

This is **not** a tax calculator.  
It is a **tax organizer** ‚Äî it prepares you to file.

## üìÑ Filing Your Taxes
You can use this organizer to manually fill in:

- **IRS Form 1040** (for 2025 tax year)  
Available at:  
https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-1040

And mail your return to the IRS using the address for your state printed on page 2 of the 1040 instructions.

Many states provide simple e-file options that are free for basic returns.
